Master of Landscape Architecture, December 2008 Graduate

As of December 2008, I have completed the MLA program at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. My MLA thesis was a combined research and design project entitled, "Landscape Heritage of Napa Valley: A Riverfront Trail and Park Design for the City of Napa, CA."

I enjoy designing at scales ranging from small scale residential to moderately sized commerical/civic projects. With a background in Art History and as an avid gardener, I have strong interests in cultural heritage and horticulture.
